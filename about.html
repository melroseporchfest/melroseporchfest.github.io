About Porchfest

Melrose Porchfest is September 28, from 2:00 pm - 6:00 pm. The rain date is September 29, from 2:00 pm - 6:00 pm.

Some common questions include...

Do I need a ticket?

No, you silly goose. The shows are free. Some musicians will put out a tip jar and you can show them some love, if you feel so inclined.

Where will the music be?

Check out the map of music for Melrose Porchfest 2019!

I have a band! How do I play?

Hey! Cool! Check out the info page for musicians and then get in touch--we have a few spots left for 2019.

I have a porch (or a driveway)! How do I host?

Please complete this form to contact us and thank you!!

I want to help! What do you need?

A community event like Porchfest runs on volunteers. And you get to hear great music all day while helping your community enjoy a fun event! The basic roles for volunteers are to help people find their way around Porchfest and to contact the Porchfest organizing committee if something's not working at a porch (like a microphone isn't working or something). Pretty easy stuff, and lots of fun. If you'd like to volunteer, please contact us at melroseporchfest@gmail.com and thank you!!

How do I get the most up-to-date information?

Please join our mailing list by emailing: melroseporchfest@gmail.com and requesting to be added. You can also give our facebook page a "like" and check out updates there.

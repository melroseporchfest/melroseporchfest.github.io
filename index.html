Schedule About Musicians For Hosts

Save the date: Saturday, September 28, 2019. 2-6pm

The Melrose Porchfest gathers musicians from in and around the Melrose community to perform on various porches and backyards. People of all ages are welcome to the many locations around town where artists will be performing in a celebration of music and our neighborhoods. It is a great way to support local artists, have fun, and enjoy our great city.

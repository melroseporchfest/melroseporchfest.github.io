Singers! Drummers! Gee-tar players! We're excited for you to help turn our little city into a celebration of music on September 29. So, thanks for your interest, and we hope this information page will give you everything you need to get going.

Fill out this easy form and we will be in touch with a spot for you to play.

You do not need to be a professional musician to play Porchfest. All skill levels are welcome.

There is no payment for playing Porchfest. You may busk by putting out a case or tip jar at your show(s) and if you have merchandise, you may put it out for sale.
